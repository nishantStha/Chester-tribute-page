About his life:

Chester Bennington was an american rock singer. He is best known for being the front man of alternative rock band Linkin Park and Dead By Sunrise. He was one of the top singers of his Genre.

Chester Bennington was born on March 20, 1976 in Phoenix, Arizona. He took interest in music at a young age, citing Depeche Mode and Stone Temple Pilots as early inspirations. His parents divorced when he was 11 years old and he started abusing Marijuana, Alcohol, Cocaine and LSD. Bennington first began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends?. He then moved on to Grey Daze. He left Grey Daze in 1998. He later joined Mike Shinoda and his band Linkin Park. The band has hits such as Numb, Faint, Crawling and In The End.
